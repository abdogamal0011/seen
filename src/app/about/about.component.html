Our Story

Tale behind Seen real state consultancy

Seen Real Estate is an international real estate consultancy that was established in 2017 in Saudi Arabia. Due to our remarkable growth and success, we have expanded our operations to Cairo, Egypt, with two branches located in New Cairo and Nasr City. Our goal is to be at the forefront of property marketing and provide tailored, innovative solutions that meet the individual needs of our clients, ensuring the best possible outcome is achieved.

With a strong presence in Saudi Arabia, including branches in Jeddah and Riyadh, and our expansion plans to the United Arab Emirates, Seen Real Estate is well-positioned to serve clients across multiple markets in the region.
